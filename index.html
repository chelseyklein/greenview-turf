About Us

Rooted in Experience

With over 30 years of experience in the sod industry—along with a lifetime in farming—owner and operator Mitchell Sims built a reputation as one of Florida’s most knowledgeable sod growers. Upon his retirement, Mitchell passed the torch to Steve Wilson, whom he had mentored for many years.

Today, Mitchell continues to offer his expertise, helping ensure the ongoing success of Greenview Turf as a locally owned, family-operated business in the Glades community. Under Steve's leadership, Greenview Turf remains committed to delivering high-quality, fresh-cut muck-bottom turf.
